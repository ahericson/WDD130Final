Overview

Purpose

Many people who are unfamiliar with Boba beverages can become confused with how to order their drinks due to the plethora of unique flavors and toppings that are provided. The Bubb’s Boba website is here to ease this perplexing problem with our guide of step-by-step ordering instructions and popular combinations that are sure to be your next delicious treat. Ordering a delicious Boba beverage no longer must be daunting or confusing to those who are trying something new, or to those who want to explore new flavors. What new Boba flavor combinations will you come to love? Find out at the Bubb’s Boba Website.

Audience

The target audience for the Bubb's Boba website is geared towards those who have little to no experience in what Boba drinks are, how to order them, or what they can consist of. This audience can range anywhere from 11 to 80 years of age! With Boba beverages being fairly inexpensive, those who are interested in trying out these types of beverages can come to this website and see what Boba drinks are all about.This website will help those in need of direction and guidance in knowing how to order boba beverages so they can get the most out of their experience trying it for the first time! From observation and experience, the daunting task of buying anything with a wide variety can cause customers exhaustion and stress. Everyone has different taste preferences, and the information on the Bubb's Boba website will help newcomers pick the combinations of flavors and textures that would best fit their palate. Because this website is for informational and advertisement purposes, this website will be available for adventurous "foodies" through desktop, laptop, and mobile devices so that they can begin their boba endeavors whenever and wherever they may be.

Typography

Heading Font: Julius Sans One

I have chosen this font because I liked how modern it looked. I felt that it matched the soothing and calm colors that were chosen for the color palette. I also liked how it would stand out on the web page due to it being in all caps and the amount of spacing in between each letter makes it broader and easy to see.

Paragraph Font: Lato

I chose this font for the paragraphs on the website because of how well it contrasts with the heading font - it is simple, easy to read, and matches the sans-serif theme comparible to the lettering in the heading.

Our Purpose

Home page

Our purpose
Bubb’s Boba website is to make this process easier for newcomers to order Boba drinks! Whatever issues you may have had with ordering, Bubb’s Boba can help you find a solution that will take away the daunting task of ordering from a large variety of choices! Trying new flavors can be fun and exciting again! With our guide and step-by-step instructions on ordering your Boba drink, you can make your own Boba beverage creation!
Why Boba?
Some people may not be familiar with Boba beverages, but the popularity of these versatile drinks is on the rise. People of all ages can enjoy this treat, and although there are many options to choose from, it is not as confusing as it may seem. If you want to learn more about Boba drinks, let us begin with where it originates.

What is Boba?

What are Boba Drinks?
Boba, also known as Tapioca Pearls, are made from Tapioca! But boba drinks do not just consist of tapioca, there are many other toppings that can be included in Boba drinks. For example, many can enjoy their boba drinks with Azuki Bean (sweet red bean), fruit flavored jellies, fruit-flavored popping boba, ice cream, cheese foam, fresh fruit, and more! These different toppings provide different sorts of textures and flavors that can be enjoyed with any drink base.
A Delicious Combination
What about the “drink” portion of boba beverages? Well, the tapioca pearls and toppings themselves are only just half of it! Boba beverages usually consist of a base beverage where the toppings are put into. These base drinks complete the entire package! Many of the drinks that can be combined with boba toppings include flavored milk tea, flavored smoothies, and flavored Icey beverages. These flavored beverages can also be ordered hot, and without any boba toppings! The variations are up to you.
The Old and the New
There are many flavors that your base beverage can come in! Some may be flavors that are already common such as vanilla, chocolate, cookies n’ cream, or strawberry. But, if you are feeling adventurous, you can also try more exotic flavors such as Taro, Red Bean, Lychee, Passion Fruit, or Honey Dew! Whichever you decide, you are sure to have a good experience!

How to Order

How do you Order?
With all this information, you might still be confused as to how to order a good boba beverage? Well, Bubb’s boba is here to help! There are many common combinations that are sure to inspire your own creativity. For instance, any flavored drink with regular tapioca pearls is usually a basic boba formula for a good beverage! Tapioca pearls themselves generally do not have a particular flavor, which makes them a great addition to any flavor drink.
The Combinations are endless!
Other exciting flavor combinations can include a Taro smoothie with Tapioca Pearls and Cheese Foam! There are other fun and delicious combinations that can be found on Bubb’s Boba website. With our already-made combinations, it may inspire you to try something new. If you would rather choose flavors and toppings on your own, we have a guide that will help you know what exactly to order, and how to order it.
